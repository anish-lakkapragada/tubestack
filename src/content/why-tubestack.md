# why tubestack

recommendation algorithms suffocate our own thoughts, tastes, and interests. while youtube's feeds may provide us infinite material, they leave us starved and unsatisfied as we never consider what we want to see.

great software is transparent and quiet. it brings out the best in its users and invites them to grow. it doesn't need autoplay and infinite scroll to keep you to stay.

i made tubestack to brings substack's intentionality to youtube. you search for content and read the cleanly formatted articles. there's no recommendation feed or click-baity thumbnails to get in your way. just a clean interface designed to help you organize your reading. moreover, tubestack is creator-focused and gives you a clean interface to plan your reading. 

this is not a new platform. it is just a calmer way to explore all which already exists. 

you know who you are and what you like. now reach for it.
